    Bio

    Genentech (2022 - Present)

    I'm working as a machine learning scientist at Prescient Design, a Genentech accelerator. My research agenda includes fundamental machine learning methods development and applications to drug discovery, particularly antibody design.


    Education (2013-2022)

    I hold a Ph.D. from the NYU Center for Data Science, advised by Professor Andrew Gordon Wilson, and a Master’s degree in Operations Research from Cornell University. I also have a Bachelor’s degree in Applied Mathematics from the University of Colorado Denver.


    General Interests

    When not occupied with research, I enjoy volleyball, rock climbing, surfing, and snowboarding.
